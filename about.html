Where did Redeor come from?

Redeor has been shaping into being for over a decade.

Redeor, a digital and creative strategy studio, which is producing work in several states, began as the brainchild of Kiki Roeder.
Launching her career as a successful business developer, Roeder translated her understanding of people into a robust marketing and communications career. This later thundered into design, user experience and web development.

Roeder moves to Atlanta and begins the three-month Web Development Immersive at General Assembly, building upon her seven years of coding experience.

April 2015

Founder Kiki Roeder leaves position as Director of Communications and Digital Strategy at the University of Tennessee to start the Redeor Studio.
